04. @notevenwali — the youtube channel

March 12, 2024 4 min read

so i started a youtube channel. it's called Not Even Wali. same as the substack. same voice. different format.

the plan is video essays. activism content. the kind of stuff i've been writing about on substack but now with a face and a voice attached to it. Nigerian politics. global movements. the things that make me sit up at my desk at 2am and start typing before i'm fully awake.

i've been thinking about doing this for a while. the substack felt like the right first step — building the writing before building the presence. figuring out what i actually want to say before worrying about how i look saying it. i think that was the right call. i know what Not Even Wali is now in a way i didn't when i first started.

video essays are a different muscle though. writing for the page and writing for the screen are not the same thing. the rhythm is different. the pacing. the way you hold someone's attention when they can look away from you at any second. i'm learning that in real time.

the activism angle matters to me specifically because there isn't enough of it in the Nigerian creator space that takes itself seriously without being preachy. i don't want to lecture. i want to think out loud in a way that makes other people want to think alongside me. that's the goal. whether i pull it off is a different question.

the channel is @notevenwali. if you're here on the portfolio you already know the vibe. come watch me figure out how to translate it into video.
