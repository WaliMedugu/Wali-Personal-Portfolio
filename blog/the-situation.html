Personal Reflections

02. the ████ situation

March 18, 2024 4 min read

my best friend got a boyfriend. and i'm happy for her. i want to be really clear about that first because everything i say after this is going to sound like i'm not and i am. genuinely. ████ deserves good things and if ████ makes her happy then i'm on ████'s side by default. but.

she's not as present anymore. and i know that's how it works. i know this is the natural order of things. i've even given this exact advice to someone else recently — step back, be her cheerleader, don't make her choose. i know the right answer. i've said the right answer out loud with my own mouth. it still feels weird.

it's not jealousy exactly. it's more like... i keep reaching for something that's slightly shifted. like rearranging a room. everything is still there but the layout is different and your muscle memory keeps failing you in small ways. you go to sit in a chair that's moved two feet to the left. nothing is wrong. everything is just different.

i miss the version of our friendship where i was getting her full attention and she was getting mine. i'm not entitled to that anymore and i know it. but missing it isn't the same as being entitled to it.

i think what i'm actually feeling is something like growing pains. friendships evolve. people find people. the ones worth keeping find a new shape that fits everyone. i believe that. i'm just not at the new shape yet. i'm still in the in between part where the old shape is gone and the new one hasn't fully settled.

she's still my person. that part hasn't moved. i just have to figure out how to be her person from a little further back than i'm used to. working on it.
